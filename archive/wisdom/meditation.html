February 25th 2014 -My meditation started out with my mind getting lost in random thoughts -Near the end, I started to get a feeling. It felt strange, like something was happening inside my mind - I also felt it in my chest. -I told myself to embrace the feeling, and not to be afraid of it or to repress it. I wanted to encourage it, because I believed *that* was the feeling of meditation -I started to become engulfed in a bright whiteness Other notes: -Prior to my meditation, I was wondering "what should meditation feel like" and I wrote it down in my todo list to look it up.
