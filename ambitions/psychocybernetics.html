Psycho Cybernetics

What is Psycho Cybernetics?

Psycho Cybernetics is a self-help book created by Maxwell Maltz, a German plastic surgeon. Maltz claims that you can achieve your goals, if:
  1. You can clearly communicate your goals to your sub-conscious
  2. You self-image is consistent with these goals.

Does it work?

Number of life goals achieved is obviously not a very scientific variable to measure. The only way I can determine if it works, is by trying it myself. Its free, and at the worst I would have spent time learning more about what is important to me, which is not a waste of time in my opinion. Also, I would like to read enough self-help material so that I can write my own book some day.

2) WHAT IS YOUR SELF-IMAGE

Positive thinking and setting goals will not work unless you view yourself as someone who can actually accomplish these goals. "But how can I believe that I'm someone who can accomplish goals that I've yet to accomplish, if I haven't accomplished them yet!?". Your brain will experience the imagining yourself achieving goals, has a similar effect on yourself self-image as actually achieving in real life. I proved this to myself by imagining the smell of certain foods can get my stomach grumbling, which is a physical effect to an imagined thought.
