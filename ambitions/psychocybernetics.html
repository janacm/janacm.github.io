Psycho Cybernetics

This is a self-help book created by a plastic surgeon, Maxwell Maltz. Maltz claims that you can achieve anything, provided the following:
  1. You can clearly communicate your goals to your sub-conscious
  2. You self-image is consistent with these goals.

This is obviously not very scientific, but can any type of self-help book ever be? Perhaps if we could monitor the changes in the brain when it comes to proponents of success, such as resilience, motivation, energy, .etc. so the only way I can determine if it works, is by trying it myself. Your brain will experience the imagining yourself achieving goals, has a similar effect on yourself self-image as actually achieving in real life.
