What is this site?

Habit Helper is a website designed to help people changing bad habits, ranging from Facebook addiction to cocaine abuse. The idea is to help people gain awareness to why and how they have their bad habits, so that they can begin to gradually take action. A lot of the time, we act on our bad habits because our brains are on auto-pilot. We try to put you back into the driver seat.
