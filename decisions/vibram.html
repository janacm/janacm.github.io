Healthy Footwear

I've heard stories about people with knee and back problems after excericsing in the wrong shoes. I want to start running every week, so I've done some research on what a "healthy" shoe is.

Results of Research

I'm going to buy Vibram Komodo Sports

Wellness Mama has done an excellent review of barefoot running, and provided academic sources for her claims.

Below is a collection of stuff I learned throughout my research. The most important takeaway from my research is DO NOT STRIKE THE GROUND HEEL FIRST WHILE RUNNING. Every single article/page I read had mentioned this fact.

How to know a shoe fits

Howe's Pharmacy has great advice:

From there website:

  • There should be a half-inch between the tip of your longest toe and the end of the shoe.
  • Try on both shoes; one foot is usually bigger than the other.
  • Make sure the ball of your foot fits easily in the widest part of the shoe. Likewise, your heel shouldn't slip around in the back of the shoe.
  • Shop in the late afternoon when your feet are slightly swollen, especially if you typically exercise after work.
